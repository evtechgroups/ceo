About

EVHEMANTH SAI is an Indian entrepreneur and technology innovator specializing in AI, software development, and cybersecurity. He founded EVTECH GROUPS in 2023, aiming to revolutionize AI-driven business solutions.

Born on November 29, 2007, in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh, India, he has made significant contributions in AI-driven technologies, EdTech, and software development.
